OUR GOAL


At ACTS ENERGY we work to reduce your business or organization's utility costs, making you more efficient and saving you money.

ABOUT US


With over 40 years of experience in the natural gas industry, we have the expertise to consult with our customers on current trends as well as opportunities to limit exposure from growing utility expenses. ACTS ENERGY can also help with budgeting your natural gas costs by locking in fixed prices for a specified period of time. We strive to make your business or other organization more energy efficient and economical, with little or no changes in your day-to-day operations.

From negotiating bids for natural gas to intervening with the utility company for the best transport rate, ACTS ENERGY will work for you to achieve your utility savings goals.
